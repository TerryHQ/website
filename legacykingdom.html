üõ† Commands ▶

Messaging

  • /message [target] [message] Sends a message to a specific player.

Land Claiming

  • /claim Claim the chunk for -$1000
  • /unclaim Unclaim and get +$1000
  • /claimShow Green = Not Claimed, Red = Others Claim, Gold = Your claim

Economy

  • /balance Shows your money.
  • /baltop See top balances.
  • /pay [target] [amount] Pay another player.

Teleporting

  • /tpa [player] Request to teleport to a player.
  • /sethome Set your home location.
  • /home Teleport to your home.
  • /spawn Teleport to spawn.
  • /wild Random teleport.
  • /back Go to last location.

Inventory

  • /shop Open shop GUI.
  • /enderchest Open enderchest GUI.
  • /workbench Open workbench GUI.
  • /clear Clear inventory.
  • /hat Put item in hand on head.

Misc

  • /suicide Kills you.
  • /rules Show rules.
  • /ping Your ping.
  • /ping [target] Player's ping.
