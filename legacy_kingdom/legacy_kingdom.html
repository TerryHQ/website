Server Facts

Eating Crimson Fungus will give the player fire resistance for 10 seconds

Right clicking a campfire with a log in hand heals nearby players

Using two iron blocks create an elevator and hit space or shift

Wearing full netherite armor gives the player fire resistance.

Right clicking a totem will add another heart to your bar.

Players can mine spawners with a diamond pickaxe

Players do not take fall damage in claimed chunks

Sapling items left on the ground will auto plant

Hostile mobs do not spawn in claimed chunks

You can wear almost any item on your head.

Fire does not spread in claimed chunks

Tnt does not ignite in claimed chunks

Players leave a grave when they die

Players can sit on stairs
